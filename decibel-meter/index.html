Decibel Meter Features

Multiple Measurement Modes

dBA, dBC, dBZ weightings plus NIOSH, OSHA, and Baby Safety modes. Choose the right measurement for your needs with industry-standard compliance.

Real-Time Monitoring

Live decibel readings with circular gauge display. Track Min, Max, and Average statistics with color-coded visual feedback and reference labels.

Advanced Frequency Analysis

Frequency spectrum analyzer with bar and line charts. Octave band analysis (1/1, 1/2, 1/3) with expandable full-screen chart view.

Time Response Settings

Fast (125ms), Slow (1000ms), Impulse (35ms), and 3s (3000ms) response times. Capture brief sounds or smooth long-term averages.

Exposure Tracking

Track noise exposure over time with dosimeter functionality. Calculate dose percentage, projected 8-hour exposure, and time to limit warnings. NIOSH and OSHA compliant.

Professional PDF Reports

Generate comprehensive reports with graphs, statistics, and exposure data. Share via email, messages, or save to Files. Perfect for workplace documentation.
